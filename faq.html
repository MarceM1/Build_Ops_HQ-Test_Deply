What is a roofing company’s #1 expense?

In the roofing industry, the number one expense for many companies is office overhead. Office overhead refers to the costs associated with running the administrative side of your roofing business. This includes expenses related to maintaining an office space, utilities, equipment, technology, administrative staff salaries, insurance, and other operational necessities.

While labor and material costs are significant factors in the roofing business, office overhead can often take the lead as the top expense.
